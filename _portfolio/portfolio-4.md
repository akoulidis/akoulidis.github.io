excerpt: "Development of a dynamic drilling simulator for advanced training and research applications. The platform was further developed from an existing framework to incorporate analytical models for rate of penetration (ROP), torque, mechanical specific energy (MSE), and bottom hole assembly (BHA) configurations, including mud motor systems. The simulator enables both rotating and sliding drilling modes and integrates data-driven drilling analytics to evaluate performance and efficiency. A key advancement is the implementation of fully dynamic simulations, where instantaneous changes in drilling parameters directly affect ROP, torque, standpipe pressure (SPP), and other key variables in real time. This capability allows realistic representation of drilling operations and supports optimization, decision-making, and training in complex drilling scenarios. Five universities have utilized it, with up to 70 licenses functioning per day.<br/><img src='/images/Payzone.png'>"
