title: "Artificial Intelligence to Predict Drilling Efficiency and Rock Strength"
excerpt: "The present study considers two additional datasets: in-cutter force sensing data and vibrations. An artificial neural network (ANN) model utilizes the in-cutter sensing data and the scaled drilling rig sensors to predict the rock strength and rate of penetration. The model employs the acquired data, derived variables, and mechanical properties of the rock samples to conduct the prediction. A scoring system
is used to evaluate the drilling efficiency by coupling the mechanical specific energy and vibration modes. The reliability of the framework is demonstrated by testing the validity of the ANN model on samples with various mechanical properties. A sensitivity analysis is conducted to optimize the ANN’s structure and determine the impact of each drilling parameter on the ANN’s performance. Furthermore, it is demonstrated that the ANN can produce accurate predictions for numerous rock samples. <br/><img src='../images/Figure 7.png'>"
